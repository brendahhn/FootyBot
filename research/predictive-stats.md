# Predictive stats — year-over-year correlation with next season's PPG

Generated by `pipeline/predictive_stats.py` from real nflverse data (see `data/raw/fetch_summary.json` for exact row counts/seasons). Minimum 6 games in both seasons required per player-season pair to reduce small-sample noise. Correlation = Pearson r between the stat in season N and points-per-game in season N+1, under this league's exact scoring formula (`pipeline/league_scoring.py`), pooled across all available season pairs.

**Known gap:** league scoring's 40+ yard run/reception bonuses and offensive fumble return TDs are NOT included (not computable from weekly aggregate data) -- every PPG figure below under-counts real league points by a small, boom-play-dependent amount.

**Not computed (data not yet available):** snap% (offense + IDP), red zone touches, yards per route run. These need `snap_counts.csv`, play-by-play red-zone splits, and participation data respectively -- none provided yet.
